Jennifer - CEO & Organizer

Born and raised in Brooklyn, NYC. Jenny as what her friends like to call her was an avid film fan. She always dreamed of becoming a movie director.

She worked on several successful indie movies and now she's now ventured into management and promotions.

She now runs Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival where she makes valuable contribution to the film culture in Brooklyn.
